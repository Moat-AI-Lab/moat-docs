4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account.
  • Upon account deletion, all Firebase Authentication and Firestore data is immediately deleted.
  • On-device portfolio data is not stored on our servers and is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.
  • Google Analytics data is automatically deleted per Google's retention policy (default 14 months).

5. Data Security

  • Passwords are one-way hashed via Firebase Authentication.
  • On-device data is encrypted via iOS Keychain / Android EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • All communications use HTTPS (TLS 1.2+).
  • Firestore security rules ensure users can only access their own data.

6. Your Rights

  • You can view, edit, or delete your portfolio data at any time within the app.
  • You can modify your profile (nickname) within the app.
  • You can delete your account and all associated server data at any time.
  • For data access, correction, or deletion requests, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for users aged 14 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 14, we will promptly delete the account and associated data.

8. International Data Transfer
